Executive Summary Dashboard

Fast, reliable leadership visibility — with drilldowns that turn questions into actions.

What it does

This dashboard gives leadership a single view of where the job stands today — schedule health, cost outlook, progress performance, and what’s blocking the next milestones. It’s designed to reduce “status meeting noise” and focus the team on priorities.

Questions it answers

  • Are we ahead or behind plan — and why?
  • Is cost trending to the forecast (or overrun)?
  • What are the top drivers impacting schedule and productivity?
  • What needs an owner today?
Best for Turnaround leadership, project managers, owners, directors, planners
Typical inputs Schedule (P6/export), cost summary, progress quantities, constraints/issues, workforce hours

What you get (deliverables)

  • Executive KPI panel + trends
  • Drilldowns by area/system/workstream
  • Top issues/constraints list with owners
  • Weekly/PDF snapshot option (if desired)

Readiness & Planning Dashboard

Readiness scoring, gating items, and upcoming milestones so teams don’t get surprised in execution.

What it does

This dashboard shows whether work is actually ready to execute — not just “scheduled.” It tracks constraints (materials, permits, access, engineering, inspection hold points), highlights upcoming risk areas, and supports clean handoffs from planning to execution.

Questions it answers

  • What is ready to release next week — and what is blocked?
  • Which constraints are trending up and need escalation?
  • Which workstreams are missing critical inputs?
  • What’s the readiness score by area/system?
Best for Planners, readiness leads, coordinators, execution leadership
Typical inputs Workpack list, constraint log, milestone dates, responsible owners, due dates

Common add-ons

  • Readiness “gates” with pass/fail criteria
  • Automatic overdue alerts by owner
  • Weekly readiness report outputs

Turnaround Wall Chart Dashboard

A “digital wall chart” that keeps the full plan aligned from planning through execution.

What it does

This is your turnaround plan in a clean, executive-ready wall chart format — but digital, filterable, and always current. It’s built to reduce confusion during fast-moving execution and keep workstreams aligned.

Questions it answers

  • What are the major gates and milestones this week?
  • Which workstreams are slipping — and what’s driving it?
  • Where is the risk concentrated (area/system)?
  • Are we on track for the next major outage milestone?
Best for Turnaround leadership, planners, execution managers, coordination meetings
Typical inputs Milestone list, workstream plan, P6 schedule export, readiness/constraint signals

Optional enhancements

  • Risk ranking and readiness heat maps
  • Gate acceptance checklist tracking
  • Printable “meeting mode” views

Daily Field Reporting Dashboard

Turns daily updates into structured reporting for faster decisions and cleaner communication.

What it does

This dashboard converts field updates into a consistent, leadership-friendly view — progress, productivity, blockers, and open issues. It reduces the time wasted chasing updates and supports better coordination across teams.

Questions it answers

  • What was accomplished today and what is planned tomorrow?
  • Where did productivity drop — and what’s the reason?
  • What blockers are repeating and need escalation?
  • Which areas are trending behind expected progress?
Best for Field supervisors, construction managers, turnaround execution, coordinators
Typical inputs Daily logs, quantity tracking, manpower, issues list, photos/notes (optional)

Common add-ons

  • Photo evidence + proof links

Workpack Control Dashboard

Track workpacks end-to-end with proof, closeout readiness, and real accountability.

What it does

This dashboard tracks workpack status from planned → released → executed → closed, with proof and closeout readiness. It creates visibility into workflow bottlenecks and helps teams stop losing time in handoffs.

Questions it answers

  • How many workpacks are ready to release right now?
  • Which workpacks are stuck and why?
  • Are we closing work with proof and documentation?
  • What’s the trend of releases vs closes?
Best for Workpack owners, coordinators, turnaround controls, planners
Typical inputs Workpack list, workflow statuses, proof attachments/links, closeout checklist items

Works with

  • SharePoint lists, Excel logs, Power Apps, Adalo
  • P6 or schedule exports (optional)

Constraints & Materials Dashboard

See what’s holding work back — prioritize actions and remove blockers before they impact schedule.

What it does

This dashboard highlights constraints that slow execution — materials, permits, access, engineering, inspections, and more. It drives accountability by showing owners, due dates, aging, and what constraints are causing the most schedule risk.

Questions it answers

  • What constraints are blocking work today?
  • Which constraints are overdue and who owns them?
  • Which constraint types are trending up?
  • What’s the aging profile and top schedule risk drivers?
Best for Coordinators, planners, procurement, engineering, execution leadership
Typical inputs Constraint log, owners, due dates, status, category/type, affected workpacks

Optional enhancements

  • Aging + root cause tracking
  • Action effectiveness (did we actually remove the blocker?)
  • Escalation lists for daily coordination
